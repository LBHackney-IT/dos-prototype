Description

We are a registered charity that manage and showcase innovative theatre projects for adults with learning disabilities in Hackney and offer opportunities for all our members to change and grow through the arts. We run a whole range of exciting and innovative projects to include:

Black Cab Theatre Company
A weekly performing arts project for people with a mild to moderate learning disability that leads to professionally led productions for the general public.

Leader lab
An accredited training programme in theatre related skills that enables people to move towards employment opportunities.

Spinning Wheel
A multi sensory movement and music project for people with severe and profound and multiple learning disabilities.

Performance company
A core ensemble of actors with mild learning disabilities who want to bring their work to a wider audience in a range of venues and performance styles.

The Triple Agency
A specialist actors agency with a talented group of actors with learning disabilities that have successfully fulfilled many professional contracts in TV, radio and theatre.

Spring Back

Spring Back is a drama project to build your resilience, have fun, make friends and shape a support network around you.

Our Mission
To advance the education of persons with learning disabilities, in particular, but not exclusively through the promotion of the arts of drama, dance and related activities, with a view to developing life skills and improving the conditions of life of such persons.
