About these prototypes

These prototypes explores how we might make information session and validation quicker and less effort in a directory of service.

There are two user groups involved in making this happen.

  1. Data stewards: who are responsible for keeping the directory of service information up-to-date
  2. Service providers: who are responsible for keeping their service information up-to-date

Data steward pages

The data steward prototypes present potential solutions for raising the visibility of out-of-date service information, which would allow a service steward to quickly take action.
